Child Dose Guidelines

Ensuring the right dose for children is critical for their safety and health. Always consult with a pediatrician before administering any medication. The dosage depends on factors such as age, weight, and medical condition. Follow these general guidelines:

  • Use a proper measuring device for liquid medications.
  • Never exceed the recommended dosage.
  • Store medications out of reach of children.
  • If unsure, consult with your healthcare provider immediately.
