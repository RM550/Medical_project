Key Features

Our GFR calculator provides an easy and accurate way to estimate kidney function based on age, gender, creatinine levels, and race. It’s scientifically backed and designed for healthcare professionals, as well as individuals wanting to monitor their kidney health.

How It Works

The calculator uses the MDRD equation to estimate your GFR. Based on your input values such as age, serum creatinine levels, gender, and race, the formula gives you an accurate GFR value in mL/min/1.73m².
